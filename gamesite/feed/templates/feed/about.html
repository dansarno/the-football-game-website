Lessons Learnt From Past Competitions

  • So called football experts rarely win this competition
  • The game is skewed towards the final. This allows for a great ending as most people may still be able to win
  • The older you become and the more football you watch, the less you really know about the game
  • For those who are not married, get married and let your partner do your entry
  • Have another child and let your one year old make the selections
  • Watch less football
  • Do not laugh at your partner or child. After they have completed their entry, copy some of their selections when they are not looking
  • Do not laugh when someone picks a team based on looks or on their favourite colour
  • Do not tease your partner or child saying they do not know anything about football because they tend to beat you
